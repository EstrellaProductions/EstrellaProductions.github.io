Take one

TAKE ONE is the introductory fest of Estrella Productions, If Celluloid is the burning heart of Estrella Productions then TAKE ONE is the spark that ignites it. Filling the hearts of freshmen with curiosity and passion for film production and making it leaves them in awe for what's next in store for Celluloid.

Celluloid

Celluloid is the three day film fest of Future Institute Of Engineering and Management -a cinematic,compact and complete celebration of movies and more.Estrella Productions can boast about it's history of Organizing Celluloid over the years with vigour and vibrance-filled with exciting events,film screenings and top celebrities gracing us with their presence.People wait for Celluloid the entire year because besides being fantastic,this fest is also an emotion for all.

Cellestra

Cellestra marks the third and final day of the fest Celluloid. An award ceremony which has been graced every year by the appearance of renowned celebrities like Abir Chatterjee, Anirban Bhattacharya, RJ Praveen and so on.

Chapter Primiera

Chapter Primiera '23 is celebrated as the foundation day of Estrella Productions. This event marked the 10th years of the togetherness as a family where people from different passion and talents is united through 'Estrella'. It is the inauguration event of Estrella Productions which gives the zeal for arranging big and successful events like Take One and Celluloid.

Panchami Parikrama

Durga puja is the biggest festival in Bengal and Estrella Productions celebrates this festival with a Photowalk. It is held on the day of Panchami. On that day club members and others college students (especially 1st years) meet near Menoka Cinema Hall and start Pandel hopping. The day spends with full of enjoyment. It becomes more memorable when we see the smile on the depriveds. Then it finishes at Madox Square.

Food-O-Graphy

A photo contest that captured the essence of Dacres Lane, Kolkata's street food hotspot. Participants froze moments of sizzling kathi rolls and aromatic chai stalls, showcasing the vibrant colors and culture of this culinary haven through their lenses.
